About me.

Work.Code.

I love working with new technology. Unfortunately, it's not always the best to take only the newest things you can get. Cool things rock, but they have to be solid. You know that, look at the new web technologies that are released every day and see how far they are away from cross-browser compatibility. That is probably the reason why I am more into backend technologies over the years. Whatever you use on the backend side, its up to you - no one is affected by the php-version or if you rather use a Tomcat or any other Java EE server.


Work.Design.

I like taking pictures. I like designing websites. I like things for the eye. Many websites are still far from today's web standard. They are clonky, non responsive - or even worse - half baked responsive. My goal is end customer satisfaction. If a customer is surfing on a site and he is astonished then I am happy.


Work.Travel.

Traveling is a main part in my life. It began with my master's degree, where I spend a good part of my life in Western Australia. I met my beautiful wife from Hong Kong there, so going back and forth, connecting the best of the two worlds - the western world and the asian world - is what defines my life. Work hard. Play hard.

Faster. Better. Stronger.
