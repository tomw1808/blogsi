Whats this about?

We think there is potential to improve significantly how people program!

We are a small team of programmers from Austria, working for companies around the world. Our experience is expressed in this article-style blog. In every article something cool will be created. The mix will always be between JS and PHP, mostly some sort of the Zend Framework will be used and probably based on that some Angular-App will be created for the Frontend.

We will look into several things:

  • Authentication and Authorization
  • Rest Interfaces
  • Extended CMS Functions.
Our aim is to show the full workflow and show how we work. We do not necessarily say that this is 100% correct, nor do we force someone to do the same. Instead we would be incredibly happy if there would be a discussion around the things and we would be happy to answer all questions. And now a not matching quote, because quotes are cool.
